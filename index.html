An Overview

Today's economy is heavily dependent upon fossil-fuels for energy usage. We see them in our cars, homes and more, and they stand as the foundation of our life. Of course though, nothing great comes without a risk. Fossil-fuels pose many harmful effects on the environemnt, and we can see the negative effects growing fast. All hope isn't lost though, as there are many actions that we can take to combat any and practically all of these issues. Throughout this website, we've compiled information from diverse data sets, and we aim to help you understand how to contribute to the cause.
