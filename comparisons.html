Geothermal Heat Pumps

Source: dandelionenergy.com

Geothermal pumps are the most efficient and clean way to power heating and cooling. They can save 30-70% on heating costs and 20-50% of cooling costs.

Solar Panels

Source: ecoflow.com

In NC, the average monthly electricity bill is $120.98, but solar panels can save on average $109.24, bringing the bill down to $11.74.

Wind Systems

Source: energy.gov

If there is enough wind in your area, small wind electric systems can be some of the most cost-effective renewable energy systems. They can lower your electricity bills by 50-90%.

Future Outlook

Source: world101.cfr.org

Cap and Trade: Cap and Trade is a government program designed to limit private sector greenhouse gas emissions. The government sets a specific number of permits that give the right to emit a specific amount of greenhouse gasses. If companies want to emit more gas, they must trade with other companies. In this way, city/county emissions can be designated to a certain amount
